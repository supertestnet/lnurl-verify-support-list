Lnurl Verify

What is lnurl verify?

Lnurl verify is an extension of the lnurl paycode protocol. An lnurl paycode is a static piece of text which contains instructions for how to request a lightning invoice from a lightning server. They are also the basis for lighting addresses. Lnurl *verify* is an extension of the paycode protocol that allows a service to check the status of an invoice it previously requested, namely, whether that invoice is paid or not.

The lnurl verify protocol is growing in usage as more lnurl servers add support. Some services also *only* work with lnurl-verify compatible lightning servers. Add support for lnurl verify and get on the "good list" of servers that support it!
